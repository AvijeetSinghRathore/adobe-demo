## 2. Adobe Space Tutorial (`adobe-space.html`)

### Tutorial Trigger Button
- **Button Text:** Take a Tour

### Tutorial Steps (Popovers)
**Step 1**
- **Title:** New PDF Space created to close deal with Acme Corp
- **Description:** Sales Rep can now leverage Acrobat PDF space to finalize an agreement using relevant context from the organization

**Step 2**
- **Title:** Integrated Knowledge Hub
- **Description:** All the relevant files, mails & Sharepoint links pertaining to Acme Corp are fetched here. Contracts from similar companies like Acme Corp and internal guidelines from Legal Team are also fetched to generate insights

**Step 3**
- **Title:** Draft Agreement Generated
- **Description:** See the details of how the draft agreement was generated so that Sales Rep is aware of the context and can make changes if needed.

**Step 4**
- **Title:** AI Insights
- **Description:** This card shares insights from the knowledge hub so that Sales Rep can learn from what others had done earlier and avoid to & fro of agreement with the client and other internal partners like Legal & Compliance teams

**Step 5**
- **Title:** Recommended Actions
- **Description:** Based on the insights, see what are some recommended actions to generate the final agreement. One click button to send it to Adobe Sign workflow

**Step 6**
- **Title:** 1-Click Prompts
- **Description:** Sales Rep can update the agreement based on the insights by just clicking on these prompts
